Research

Selected papers and projects.

Behavioral Attenuation and Teachers’ Take-up of EdTech

RCT · Rural China

Randomized controlled trial with 138 middle school English teachers and ~16,000 students evaluating behaviorally-informed interventions to improve teacher adoption of Duolingo and downstream teaching/learning outcomes.

  • Control vs low-dosage vs high-dosage interventions targeting information-processing constraints
  • Both treatments increased daily usage; high-dosage showed the highest engagement
  • High-dosage increased mentions of other apps by 11pp (suggesting spillovers)
